Molly & Rosie
A Story of Messy Hair & Big Dreams

For Every Girl
with Dirt on Her Knees
and Stars in Her Eyes

This is the mostly-true, slightly-embellished, completely heartwarming story of a girl who was born in a hurry, grew up in a whirlwind, and found her soul's companion on four furry legs.

Buckle up. She doesn't slow down.
